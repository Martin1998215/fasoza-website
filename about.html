Our Objectives

  • To foster cause for conservation of the aquatic environment in Zambia in line with national development policies.
  • To create linkages between different players in the fisheries and aquaculture sectors.
  • To engage in research and extension program related to fisheries and aquaculture.
  • To participate in social activities that develops the studentâ€™s responsibility towards the communities and the environment.
  • To create and maintain a high standard of qualification, competence and integrity in persons engaged in all aspects fisheries and aquaculture practices in Zambia.
  • To advance public awareness of the beneficial influence of fisheries and aquaculture in relation to conservation of aquatic environment and the role of fisheries and aquaculture development through field excursions for schools, higher institutions of learning and the public, and through all available media as the conservation of the society shall permit.

HOW TO BECOME A MEMBER

FASOZA is open to anyone with the zeal and interest in our objectives and aims as a society. FASOZA membership is based on a calender year from 1st January to 31st December and these members are grouped in the following categories:

FULL MEMBERS

These include students from the Copperbelt University in the School of Natural Resources who have fully paid the subscription fee which will be determined by the society for that particular year.

ASSOCIATE MEMBERS

These include all members who show interest in the promoting and developing the society. They are also however required to pay subscription fee as their contribution will be highly appreciated by the society.

HONORARY LIFE MEMBERS

These are members selected by the Executive with the approval of at least 75% of the fully paid up members of the society, and will include individuals with prominent and outstanding contributions to the society; such members shall be exempted from payments of subscriptions and member who has served the society for ten (10) or more years.

AFFILIATE MEMBERS

Membership will only be open to groups, institutions, organizations or networks. No subscription fees will be required from these, this membership will however not be extended to the individuals within those groups, organizations, institutions or networks.

INDIVIDUAL MEMBERS

Membership will consist of interested individuals which include students other than those from the School of Natural Resources. These shall be required to pay subscription fees as determined by the executive.

PATRON

This member will be a lecturer at the School of Natural Resources of the Copperbelt University and will be elected from the members of the society and will serve for two (2) years.
